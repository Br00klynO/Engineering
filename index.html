8/21-8/24 Week 1
This week we learned about Safety and Etiquette, and did our first challenge. We learned
that while "in the workshop" it is important to follow S.O.P.s, the standard operating
procedure. It is also important to make sure you are wearing the correct equipment and
clothing to be safe. In case of emergency, there are safety features such as the eye wash
station. After learning about safety, we did a challenge. In the challenge our goal was
to transport a marble from the starting point to a bucket using only 7 half tubes. To do
this, we lined up the tubes holding them at slight slopes, and then had everybody who was
not holding a tube line up in front of the last tube. We would then pass on the tubes when
the marble had already gone through them. Using this strategy, we were able to get the
marble to the location of the bucket, we just had trouble actually getting it in. I think
if we had started off holding our pipes lower, we would have been able to do it.
